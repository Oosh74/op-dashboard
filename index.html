The Great Houses

House Atreides

Homeworld: Caladan ║ Status: Active ║ Allegiances: Loyal to Emperor ║ Rivalries: House Harkonnen

Noble and just, House Atreides is renowned for its honor, military acumen, and the loyalty it inspires in subjects and soldiers alike. Though once lords of the oceanic world of Caladan, the Atreides were granted stewardship of Arrakis—the sole source of spice melange—marking a pivotal shift in their political influence. Duke Leto Atreides is known for his fairness and integrity, though such virtues are perilous in the Imperium\'s shadowed game.

“There is no call we do not answer, there is no faith that we betray.”

House Harkonnen

Homeworld: Giedi Prime ║ Status: Active ║ Allegiances: CHOAM-Aligned ║ Rivalries: House Atreides

Ruthless and cunning, House Harkonnen thrives on fear and domination. Their rule over Arrakis prior to House Atreides was marked by brutal exploitation and a singular focus on profit. Baron Vladimir Harkonnen\'s influence stretches far beyond his corpulent frame, and his house remains a symbol of raw ambition and moral corruption in the eyes of many.

"Death comes.”

House Vernius

Homeworld: Ix ║ Status: Restored (under oversight) ║ Allegiances: Nominally Loyal, Tleilaxu Influence ║ Rivalries: House Corrino, Technocratic Factions

Once one of the most technologically advanced houses in the Imperium, House Vernius of Ix suffered political disgrace and exile. Though technically restored, the house now operates under heavy scrutiny and influence from the Tleilaxu and CHOAM. Ixian technology remains coveted, and their innovations often skirt the edge of the anti-AI proscriptions of the Butlerian Jihad.

Maxim: “Innovation knows no bounds but secrecy.”

House Richese

Homeworld: Richese ║ Status: Active ║ Allegiances: CHOAM-Aligned ║ Rivalries: House Vernius (technological rivalry)

Quietly powerful, House Richese produces finely crafted mechanical devices and optics sought across the Imperium. More discreet than Ix, their wealth and influence flow through innovation rather than force.

“Precision is the soul of mastery.”

House Corrino

Homeworld: Salusa Secundus ║ Status: Reigning ║ Allegiances: Emperor of the Known Universe ║ Rivalries: House Atreides, House Vernius

The ruling House of the Imperium, House Corrino sits atop the Lion Throne with the Padishah Emperor Shaddam IV as its current sovereign. Maintaining a delicate balance of power among the Great Houses and CHOAM, they command the fearsome Sardaukar legions, trained on the prison world Salusa Secundus. Their rule is absolute, but not unchallenged.

“Order must be enforced through fear.”
